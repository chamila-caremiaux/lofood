Potatoes


LoFood potatoes are fairly large and oblong shaped. These potatoes are best when baked, fried, mashed, or used in casseroles. They are organically grown without harmful pesticides, herbicides and fertilisers. Our farmers use traditional earth-friendly farming methods inspected by a nationally recognised agency to verify organic authenticity. Organic potatoes are preferred for reasons of taste, health and the environment. To preserve the nutrients in these wonderfully healthy treats, leave the skins on and simply scrub gently in water before using. Store in a cool, well-ventilated area rather than refrigerate. Organic vegetables should not be stacked with non-organic vegetables. At Gally’s Farms, we defend a link with the nature. Our point of view is simple: the nature does well, we need to do the rest. We cultivate our products and invent our services which permitted to the city and its inhabitants to preserve a precious link with the benefits of a natural environment. We have a stock farming, a dairy, and a fruit and vegetable pick-your-own. Everything we produce is organic without the use of pesticides of any kind.
