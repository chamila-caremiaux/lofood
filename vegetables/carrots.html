Carrots


Our organic carrots are deliciously sweet, enjoyable, and also free of harmful pesticides, herbicides and fertilisers. LoFood certified organic products are free of artificial or synthetic fertilisers and full of unforgettable, great tasting flavour. Organic carrots are preferred for reasons of taste, health and the environment. Delicious to eat raw or cooked, LoFood carrots are so easy to use. There is no need to peel, cut or even chop. A light rinsing is all that necessary to enjoy them. As an outstanding source of Vitamin A, LoFood carrots provide a healthy, extraordinary flavorful snack. LoFood carrots are hand packaged for excellent quality. Store in the refrigerator’s vegetable bin or after opening, in a plastic bag for up to 14 days. Since 1954, our family established the Viltain’s farm which is composed of a stock farming, a dairy, a fruit and vegetable pick-your-own and a market located in Jouy-en-Josas at 17km from Paris. Our farm is successful because of the quality of our products and the attractiveness of locally produced food in France.
