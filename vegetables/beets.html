Beets


These deep red-colored beets are the most common and are wonderfully versatile. LoFood beets are 100% natural and organic. Our farmers use traditional earth-friendly farming methods inspected by a nationally recognised agency to verify organic authenticity. Organic beets are preferred for better taste, health, and environmental progress. For the best taste, cook beets whole with one or two inches of stem intact (so that the beets do not bleed). Gently scrub and do not slice until after they have cooked. Red beets are most often times pickled and can be served hot or cold. LoFood beets are hand-selected for excellent quality. Refrigerate unwashed beets with stem and root attached. Cover for up to one week. Organic vegetables should not be stacked with non-organic vegetables. At Gally’s Farms, we defend a link with the nature. Our point of view is simple: the nature does well, we need to do the rest. We cultivate our products and invent our services which permitted to the city and its inhabitants to preserve a precious link with the benefits of a natural environment. We have a stock farming, a dairy, and a fruit and vegetable pick-your-own.
