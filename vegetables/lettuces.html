Lettuces


The consumption of lettuce is rising among consumers. Perhaps it is the nutritional benefits or the convenience of this vegetable. LoFood lettuces are 100% natural and organic. These delicious bunches of lettuce are organically grown and free of artificial or synthetic fertilisers and full of unforgettable, great tasting flavour. Our farmers use traditional earth-friendly farming methods inspected by a nationally recognised agency to verify organic authenticity. Enjoy it in salads or on sandwiches, also great in cooking, it may be steamed, braised and added to soups. LoFood Lettuces are hand-selected for excellent quality. Wrap in a towel, store in an airtight container and keep refrigerated for up to five days. Organic produce should not be stacked with non-organic produce. At Gally’s Farms, we defend a link with the nature. Our point of view is simple: the nature does well, we need to do the rest. We cultivate our products and invent our services which permitted to the city and its inhabitants to preserve a precious link with the benefits of a natural environment. We have a stock farming, a dairy, and a fruit and vegetable pick-your-own.
