Radishes


This elongated organic radishes has a distinctive scarlet colouring with a tapered white tip. The French radishes are mild and sweet, without the sharp bite of a regular radish. Slice or grate into salads; include whole on vegetable platters to add a splash of colour. The leaves are also edible. They can be cooked up like greens or added to salads for sweet, slightly peppery, flavour. They are organically grown without harmful pesticides, herbicides and fertilisers. Our farmers use traditional earth-friendly farming methods inspected by a nationally recognised agency to verify organic authenticity. Store radish whole in the home refrigerator for up to two weeks. Since 1954, our family established the Viltain’s farm which is composed of a stock farming, a dairy, a fruit and vegetable pick-your-own and a market located in Jouy-en-Josas at 17km from Paris. Our farm is successful because of the quality of our products and the attractiveness of locally produced food in France. Everything we produce is organic without the use of pesticides of any kind.
