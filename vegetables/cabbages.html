Cabbages


Although many may shirk at even the thought of cabbage, cabbage is actually a staple in other cultures and readily available in all countries. Cabbage contains a lot of nutrients and is the original weight loss food. One cup of cabbage only contains around 15 calories. These delicious heads of cabbage are organically grown without the use of harmful fertilisers, pesticides, or herbicides. Our farmers use traditional earth-friendly farming methods inspected by a nationally recognised agency to verify organic authenticity. Besides traditional cole slaw, this vegetable can be eaten raw, shredded, boiled, steamed, or even fried! There is no need to be intimidated by it, add them to salads, soups, and tacos for a refreshing change. Cabbages are available in green or red. LoFood cabbages are hand-selected for excellent quality. Keep refrigerated and unwashed for up to two weeks. Organic produce should not be stacked with non-organic produce. At Gally’s Farms, we defend a link with the nature. Our point of view is simple: the nature does well, we need to do the rest. We cultivate our products and invent our services which permitted to the city and its inhabitants to preserve a precious link with the benefits of a natural environment. We have a stock farming, a dairy, and a fruit and vegetable pick-your-own. Everything we produce is organic without the use of pesticides of any kind.
