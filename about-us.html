About Us

We started everything in 2020

LoFood

Everything began this year. We noticed that more and more people wanted products of quality especially regarding food. People do not trust anymore supermarkets food and want to buy their food directly at the source. However, farms are usually not located in the cities which make them difficult to reach. Consequently, LoFood has the solution. We connect you directly with the best farmers of your region. So, that you can buy everything you want. We deliver your order directly at your home within 2 business days.

Everything we offer is organic. Our farmers make sure to not use fertisers and pesticides during the growing process of their fruits and vegetables. We also offer a food with a quality superior compared to the one in the usual supermarkets.
