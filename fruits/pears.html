Pears


LoFood organic pears have a long tapering neck, and earthy brown skin which is oftentimes mottled. Pears make a healthy and delicious snack, but they are also good poached or baked whole. Our farmers use traditional earth-friendly farming methods inspected by a nationally recognised agency to verify organic authenticity. To ripen store pears at room temperature for up to one week. Pears may be refrigerated to stop ripening process. Organic fruits should not be stacked with non-organic fruits. At Gally’s Farms, we defend a link with the nature. Our point of view is simple: the nature does well, we need to do the rest. We cultivate our products and invent our services which permitted to the city and its inhabitants to preserve a precious link with the benefits of a natural environment. We have a stock farming, a dairy, and a fruit and vegetable pick-your-own. Everything we produce is organic without the use of pesticides of any kind.
