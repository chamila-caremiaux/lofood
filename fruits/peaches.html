Peaches


With their sweet, tender flesh and slightly fuzzy skin, organic peaches offer a wide range of uses other than eating out of hand. Try poaching them in red wine for an easy dessert or using them in a savoury salad for a unique side dish. LoFood organic peaches are naturally fresh and juicy and are grown without the use of synthetic fertilisers, pesticides, or herbicides. Our farmers use traditional earth-friendly farming methods inspected by a nationally recognised agency to verify organic authenticity. Organic peaches are preferred for their succulent, juicy taste, as well as their contribution to personal health and the environment. Store at room temperature for up to one week. Organic Peaches may be refrigerated to stop the ripening process. At Gally’s Farms, we defend a link with the nature. Our point of view is simple: the nature does well, we need to do the rest. We cultivate our products and invent our services which permitted to the city and its inhabitants to preserve a precious link with the benefits of a natural environment. We have a stock farming, a dairy, and a fruit and vegetable pick-your-own.
