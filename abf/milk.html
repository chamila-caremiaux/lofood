Milk


Our milk comes straight from the farm, delivered fresh to your doorstep in a few hours. Every drop is pure wholesome goodness. Our farmers use traditional earth-friendly farming methods inspected by a nationally recognised agency to verify organic authenticity. When we say ‘organic milk’, we mean that the milk is obtained from pasture-fed cows that are not treated with any antibiotics. We ensure that you get the best organic milk and for that, we have our own farms where we nurture the cows with love. We don’t restrict them from grazing, roaming, eating, sleep-ing or getting massages (Yes, they are our family and we pamper them). We feed them with organically cultivated seasonal greens, which are highly nutritious. This, in turn, ensures that the organic cow’s milk obtained has a high nutrition value. Since 1954, our family established the Viltain’s farm which is composed of a stock farming, a dairy, a fruit and vegetable pick-your-own and a market located in Jouy-en-Josas at 17km from Paris. Our farm is successful because of the quality of our products and the attractiveness of locally produced food in France.
